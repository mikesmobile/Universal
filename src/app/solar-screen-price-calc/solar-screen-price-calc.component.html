How to Measure

Measure from the inside of the window and round to the nearest whole foot.

Example: if your window is 3 feet and 4 inches wide, you would put 4ft for width.

What is grid?

Grid is the bars across some windows, like the one in picture on left.

We recomend no grid on screens. They float a few inches above window so the grids will look like they don't match unless your looking at window straight on.
